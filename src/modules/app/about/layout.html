about

  Corey has worked in local government and municipal GIS for 7 years and currently works for the City of Raleigh, NC Public Utilities GIS group. Corey leads this team in web application development and programming, most notably in the development of a department wide project tracking application. He began his career in GIS working for San Antonio Water Systems in 2007, and later worked 3 years for the City of Asheville, NC IT GIS group. Corey is currently in his second to last semester at NC State University where he is receiving a Masters of Geospatial Information Science and Technology (MGIST). Corey is an avid javascript developer and civic hacker who enjoys staying current in the rapidly changing world of web development and working to find new innovative ways to bring GIS to the web.
